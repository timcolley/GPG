Preview Panel

Checking your selections before building a grid.

Author

Tim Colley


The preview panel displays useful information about the tour you have most recently selected. While this is a very basic viewer, it does show several key pieces of information.

  • Tour code
  • The date of every (selected) departure
  • The price of the deparure
  • The deposit for the departure
  • and the single supplement price for the departure





Usefully, if the departure is sold out, then the price will be displayed as 'SOLD OUT'. This will also be reflected in the final price grid. If the departure features a 'No Single Supplement' offer, the single supplement will read as £0.

You can use the view to confirm pieces of information are correct and as a point of reference for brochure production as well as making sure you get all of the detail in the final price grid.
